Privacy summary

Privacy by default

CannyMinute is designed to reduce impulse buying with on-device processing. We do not sell personal data. We do not store raw screen text unless you explicitly turn on diagnostics in the app.

What we collect on this website

  • Beta waitlist fields you submit: name, email, and optional shopping context.
  • Technical metadata for anti-spam and troubleshooting, like submit timestamp.

How we use it

  • To send beta invitations, launch updates, and occasional product feedback requests.
  • To understand rough demand and improve onboarding.

Your choices

  • You can request removal from the beta list at any time.
  • You can unsubscribe from emails using the unsubscribe link in messages.
  • You can disable app protection at any time in app settings.

For data requests, contact: hello@cannyminute.com
