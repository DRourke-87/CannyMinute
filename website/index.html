How CannyMinute works

1. Detect checkout

Uses Android Accessibility to detect likely checkout signals in apps and browsers.

2. Run a quick check

Shows a fixed 30-second cooldown in Beta and three practical questions before buying.

3. Keep you in control

Continue, pause for one hour, or allow-list apps. Premium adds stricter and scheduled modes.

Trust first

Privacy by default

CannyMinute processes signals on your phone. Nothing is sold. Nothing is sent off-device unless you explicitly opt in to diagnostics.

  • Accessibility permission explained in plain English
  • Diagnostics mode is optional and off by default
  • Temporary bypass and allow-list always available

FAQ

Why does CannyMinute need Accessibility?

It is required to detect checkout screens in third-party apps and browsers so the pause screen appears at the right moment.

What data is stored?

Settings and local event decisions. Screen text is not stored unless diagnostics is turned on by you.

Can I pause or disable protection?

Yes. You can toggle protection off, use a temporary bypass, or allow-list specific apps.

What if it triggers at the wrong time?

You can continue after the cooldown, pause protection for one hour, and help tune behaviour through optional diagnostics. Save for later is planned for Premium.
